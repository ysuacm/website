Welcome to the YSU ACM!

The Youngstown State University student chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery (YSU ACM) hosts weekly meetings with presentations by students, faculty, staff, alumni, and sometimes special guests. Presentations cover all aspects of computer science and related fields. Examples include presentations on ethical hacking, command-line/shell tricks, functional programming, bitcoin, and spreadsheet hacks to impress your supervisor.
